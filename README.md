# 📘 Zerobits Studio — Work Policy

Welcome to **Zerobits Studio**.
This document outlines our work culture, expectations, and policies to ensure a smooth and professional working environment.

---

## 🕒 Working Hours

* Standard working hours: **10:00 AM – 6:00 PM (IST)**
* Flexible work culture is encouraged, provided deliverables are met on time
* Employees are expected to be available during core working hours for collaboration

---

## 🏡 Work Mode

* 100% **Remote (Work From Home)**
* Maintain a **quiet and professional work environment**
* Ensure minimal background noise during meetings

---

## 📅 Leaves & Time Off

* **Sunday is a fixed off**
* **2 additional leaves per month** (No questions asked)
* Inform your manager/team **in advance before taking leave**
* Emergency leaves should be communicated as early as possible

---

## 💰 Salary & Payments

* Salary will be credited on the **6th of every month**
* Salary cycle: **1st to 30th/31st**
* A valid **bank account is mandatory**

---

## 📈 Salary Increment Policy

* Zerobits Studio follows an **auto salary increment policy** based on roles and performance
* Increment structure will be **clearly communicated at the time of hiring**
* Example:

  * Managerial roles: **₹3,000 increment every 6 months**
* Increment policies may vary depending on role, contribution, and company growth

---

## 💻 Equipment & Requirements

You must have:

* A **personal laptop** (mandatory)
* A **stable high-speed internet connection**
* A **good quality headphone with mic** (clear audio required)

Additional requirement:

* **MacBook is mandatory for iOS developers**

⚠️ Note:

* Zerobits Studio will **not compensate for hardware issues or failures**
* You are solely responsible for maintaining your equipment

---

## 🌐 Internet Reimbursement

* Partial reimbursement may be provided for **WiFi expenses** (case-by-case basis)

---

## 🧠 Learning & Development

* Participation in:

  * Webinars
  * Virtual summits
  * Learning sessions

is **encouraged and supported**

---

## 🗣 Communication

* All official communication will be conducted via:

  * **Email**
  * **Slack**
* Employees are expected to:

  * Respond within reasonable time
  * Maintain professional communication

---

## 📊 Monitoring & Productivity

* A monitoring tool (**Hubstaff**) will be used for **non-managerial roles**
* This ensures transparency and productivity tracking

---

## 📆 Meetings

* **Weekly team meetings** will be conducted
* Additional meetings may be scheduled based on project needs
* Attendance is expected unless prior notice is given

---

## 🤝 Work Ethics & Expectations

* Maintain **professionalism and accountability**
* Deliver tasks within agreed timelines
* Communicate blockers proactively
* Respect team members and maintain a collaborative environment

---

## 🔒 Confidentiality

* All project data, client information, and internal discussions are **strictly confidential**
* Sharing sensitive information outside the organization is prohibited

---

## ⚠️ Additional Notes

* Employees are expected to manage their time effectively in a remote setup
* Consistent underperformance or misconduct may lead to disciplinary action
* Policies may evolve as the company grows

---

## 🚀 Final Note

At Zerobits Studio, we believe in **flexibility, ownership, and growth**.
We aim to build a culture where individuals take responsibility, learn continuously, and contribute to meaningful products.
